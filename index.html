Coin Metrics is a leading provider of crypto asset market and network data. Our data empowers our clients and the public to better understand, value, use, and ultimately steward open crypto networks.

Coin Metric's Products and Services

CM Network Data Pro

The ultimate dataset for analyzing crypto assets

Up to 100 assets and 200 metrics

Includes novel and adjusted metrics designed to remove artifacts and enhance insights

Aggregated daily or hourly

Delivered via REST API in JSON or CSV

Dedicated consulting,support and more

CM Network Data Community

Get immediate and actionable insights.

Access CM’s community API, download our historical data files or build custom charts for all our available assets.

Want to know more? Check our research, review our methods or join our cryptoeconomics forum and help the crypto community learn and grow together.

Market Data Feed

Get real-time data and fixed reference rates for your financial institution, trading desk or data application.

Sourced from the world’s leading exchanges, CM’s market data feed supports a growing list of assets and fiat pairs all backed by methods and standards set by traditional finance.
